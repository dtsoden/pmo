Privacy Policy for PMO Time Tracker Extension

Last Updated: November 29, 2024

Overview

PMO Time Tracker is a Chrome extension designed to work with self-hosted installations of the PMO Platform application. This extension is open-source software that organizations download, install, and host on their own infrastructure.

The extension developer does NOT collect, store, or have access to any user data.

Developer Data Collection

This extension communicates exclusively with PMO Platform instances that are:

  • Hosted by the installing organization on their own servers
  • Configured by the end user during extension setup
  • Controlled entirely by the organization, not the developer

Data Handled by the Extension

When you use this extension, the following data is processed locally on your device and transmitted only to your organization's self-hosted PMO Platform backend:

  • Authentication tokens: JWT tokens for maintaining your session with your organization's PMO Platform
  • User profile information: Your name, email, and role as stored in your organization's PMO Platform
  • Timer data: Project/task assignments and time tracking entries you create
  • Timer shortcuts: Quick-start buttons you configure for frequently used projects

All of this data:

  • Is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API
  • Is transmitted ONLY to the PMO Platform API URL you configure
  • Is controlled by your organization's PMO Platform administrators
  • Is NEVER sent to the extension developer or any third party

Your Organization's Responsibility

Because PMO Platform is self-hosted software, your organization controls:

  • What data is collected and stored
  • Where that data is hosted
  • Who has access to that data
  • How long that data is retained
  • Compliance with applicable privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

For questions about how your data is handled, contact your organization's PMO Platform administrators.

Open Source

This extension's source code is publicly available on GitHub. You can review exactly what data the extension handles and how it's used:

https://github.com/DavidSoden/CNX-PMO

Third-Party Services

This extension does NOT use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. It does not communicate with any servers except the PMO Platform backend URL you configure.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in the extension's functionality. Updates will be posted to this page with a new "Last Updated" date.

Contact

For questions about this extension:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/DavidSoden/CNX-PMO/issues

For questions about how your data is handled by your organization's PMO Platform installation, contact your organization's administrators.
